Spice Girls +1 Careers

Work Right Where You Belong

At Spice Girls +1, we promise our guests a unique dining experience and “A New Exciting Way To Eat”. And to do that, we need you – talented, engaged and passionate team members. You will quickly learn that joining Spice Girls +1 is not just your regular restaurant job. Here, you join a mission to make our guests’ eating experience extraordinary. The key to our success is simple – it’s our ability to hire the right people, train and develop them properly, and keep them happy.

How we accomplish this is what we believe sets us apart — we recognize that the growth and development of our chefs and team members is a process that never ends and that improvement is always possible. You can count on competitive compensation and benefits package, but most importantly, you will be embarking on a rewarding career with numerous growth opportunities as our company grows.

Chef

Job description About: Here at the Spice Girls+1 we believe that food makes us better. Are you interested in providing a world-class experience? Join us for the best ride of your career and be a part of the Spice Girls+1 crew!
We need a dedicated Chef who has gained considerable experience working in a high volume - fast paced kitchen. The Chef will oversee all kitchen operations with a strong knowledge of recipe development, food costing, inventory, scheduling, labour management and the development and leadership of line level talent. They will create, demonstrate and promote a kitchen culture that revolves around the passion and enjoyment of cooking, ingredients and working as a team.
Responsibilities:
- Demonstrate and communicate the vision to all crewmembers and lead by example
- Build and maintain relationships with crewmembers both back and front of house
- Present an influential presence in the kitchen which fosters growth, learning and development
- Work with the crewmembers to meet all projected goals when it comes to waste and labour control
- Oversee ordering of inventory and daily prep work
- Work on line with the crewmembers, maintaining a high level of food quality
- Oversee kitchen operations and cleanliness in compliance with health and safety regulations
- Perform all back of house crew duties as needed including prep and supportive work, working all line
stations and expediting dishes
Qualifications:
- Applicants must have 5+ years experience cooking in a full-service high volume restaurant
- Previous experience of 3-4 years in back of house management experience
- A culinary diploma or degree is considered an asset
- Red seal certification is also considered an asset
- Must possess a passion for the culinary experience and the food service industry
- Ability to take direction, work calmly under pressure and delegate tasks and provide direction as needed to team members
- Must work well with others, enjoy teaching crewmembers, and helping grow and develop top line talent
- Must possess a desire for personal and professional development.
- Eligibility to work in Canada
